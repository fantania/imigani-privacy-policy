IMIGANI APP — PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: November 22, 2025

Imigani is a storytelling mobile application designed to help users listen to and enjoy Kinyarwanda stories. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information related to your use of the app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Imigani does not collect, store, or process any personal information.

We do not collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Payment details
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics

Your use of the app is completely anonymous.

2. Donations

The Imigani app allows users to make voluntary donations to support the project.

Donations are processed securely through Stripe or a similar trusted payment provider.

Imigani does not store or access your payment information. All payment data is handled exclusively by the payment provider under their own security and compliance standards (PCI-DSS).

3. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services for:

  • Payments (e.g., Stripe)
  • Basic crash reporting from Flutter or app stores

These third parties may collect limited technical information required for their service to function, but Imigani itself does not receive or control this data.

You can review Stripe’s Privacy Policy here:
https://stripe.com/privacy

4. Children’s Privacy

The app is safe for all ages. Since we do not collect personal information, the app is compliant with COPPA and other child privacy regulations.

5. Data Security

Because we do not collect or store personal data, there is no user data exposed to risk within our system.

However, we encourage users to:

  • Use secure internet connections when making donations
  • Keep their device protected

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date at the top.
